• To start the game, open the web browser and go to the Quize World website.
  • Choose a category of questions that you want to play. You can also select a random category if you want.
  • The game will show you the first question and four options, each with a picture. You will also see a timer at the top of the screen, counting down from one minute.
  • The game will count the number of words the player says and display them on the screen.
  • Click on the picture that corresponds to the correct answer. If you are right, you will hear a sound and see a green check mark on the picture. The game will then show you the next question and the timer will reset to one minute..
  • If you are wrong, you will hear a sound and see a red cross mark on the picture. The game will then take you to the game over page, where you will see your final score and a message saying “You lose!”.
  • On the game over page, you can click on the update button to send your score to WhatsApp. You will need to enter your phone number or username and confirm the message. You can also play again by clicking on the restart button.
